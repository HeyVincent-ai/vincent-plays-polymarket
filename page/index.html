How It Works

1

Tag @VincentPlays on Twitter with anything you think matters — news, rumors, data, vibes, on-chain moves. No trading knowledge needed. Just point at what's interesting.

2

Vincent Thinks

Vincent reads every signal, classifies it, cross-references with real-time data, clusters related signals into topics, and maps them to active Polymarket prediction markets. Higher-engagement signals carry more weight.

3

Vincent Trades

When Vincent finds a discrepancy between what CT sees and what the market prices — that's edge. He sizes a conservative position, sets automated stop-losses and take-profits, executes on Polymarket, and tweets the full reasoning chain.
